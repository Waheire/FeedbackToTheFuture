We strive to create a dynamic equilibrium between the social foundation and ecological ceiling as we aim to uplift local communities.

Our Organization

We are dedicated to promoting resilient and equitable communities in Kenya and East Africa by adopting regenerative and circular economy practices. Our primary objective is to enhance the health and well-being of both people and the planet by increasing access to diverse nutritious organic foods, sustainable energy, waste management systems, natural building materials, and ecosystem restoration. We firmly believe that effective collaboration and partnership building with stakeholders from all sectors is crucial to achieving sustainable development that balances social equity and ecological preservation. We are committed to creating a regenerative economy prioritizing sustainable community development, biodiversity conservation, and environmental stewardship

Living Lab Approach

At our organization, we value community engagement and believe that involving local communities in identifying issues, brainstorming solutions, and executing improvements is key. We use a living lab approach that creates an environment where experts and community members can work together to undertake community based projects that are tailored to their specific needs.

Our living labs are spaces for creativity and experimentation of regenerative and circular innovations in real life, allowing us to test, prototype, pilot, and scale proven and working technologies or innovations. This way we ensure the full participation of different stakeholders and our programs are contextualized to the local situation.

Our Vision

Our vision is a regenerative and circular economy in an equitable world, ensuring equal access to resources and living in harmony with nature through collaboration and informed choices.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and empower individuals and communities to create a regenerative and circular economy, promoting equal access to resources, fostering collaboration, and preserving biodiversity through informed choices and actions, ultimately leading to a sustainable and equitable world.
