Our Services

Capacity
Building

At Feedback to the Future, we provide knowledge through our blended learning training, utilizing both face-to-face classes and our online training tool. Our training materials are accessible through our app, which can be downloaded

The app is designed to be accessible even without an internet connection, allowing users to access the training materials anytime, anywhere. Additionally, the app can be easily shared with others via Bluetooth connectivity, enabling knowledge sharing and dissemination within communities. Through this blended learning approach, we aim to make our training accessible, interactive, and convenient for individuals and communities seeking to learn about regenerative agriculture and sustainable practices.

Technical
Support

At Feedback to the Future, we provide technical support in setting up syntropic design systems. Syntropic design is a regenerative agricultural approach that involves creating diverse and mutually beneficial plant communities. It aims to mimic natural ecosystems, enhancing biodiversity, soil health, and productivity.

Our technical support includes guidance on on-site assessment, design planning, and implementation of syntropic design systems. We work closely with farmers and landowners to understand their specific needs and goals. Our team of experts provides advice on selecting suitable plant species, designing planting layouts, and managing the system over time

Water
Management

At Feedback to the Future, we offer community water management as a service to empower and support local communities in effectively managing their water resources. Our approach involves providing comprehensive assistance and expertise to communities in developing and implementing sustainable water management strategies.

As part of our service, we conduct assessments to understand the specific water needs and challenges of the community. We then work closely with community members, local leaders, and stakeholders to co-design and implement customized water management plans

We use a system approach to promote community development.
